Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find the answer you need? Our team is always happy to help.

Call Us Now
Is the initial consultation really free?
Yes, absolutely! Your first consultation with our dentist is completely free of charge. This includes a brief oral examination and a discussion of your dental goals. Any X-rays or detailed diagnostics will be quoted transparently before proceeding.
Do you offer payment plans for expensive treatments?
Yes! We offer flexible 0% interest payment plans for treatments above ₹15,000. We also accept all major insurance plans and work directly with your insurer to maximize your benefits. Our finance team will walk you through all options.
How long do veneers and implants typically last?
With proper care, porcelain veneers last 10–15 years and can be refreshed thereafter. Dental implants, when placed correctly and maintained well, are designed to last a lifetime — many of our implant patients from 2010 still have their original implants in perfect condition.
Is teeth whitening safe for sensitive teeth?
We offer sensitivity-specific whitening protocols using lower-concentration gels combined with desensitizing agents. Most patients with sensitivity tolerate our gentle whitening program very well. We'll assess your sensitivity level before recommending any whitening treatment.
What age should children first visit a dentist?
We recommend scheduling a child's first dental visit when their first tooth appears — usually around 6 months — or by their first birthday at the latest. Early visits help establish good habits, detect issues early, and make children comfortable with dental care from a young age.
Do you offer emergency dental care?
Yes! We provide emergency dental care 7 days a week for existing patients. If you have a dental emergency — severe pain, broken tooth, lost filling, or swelling — call our emergency line at +91 98765 00000 and we'll see you the same day.
